About Materials

Materials is a UK-based multi-genre music collective. We are a group of musicians, producers, and DJs who are passionate about music and creating a community of like-minded individuals. Our goal is to bring people together through music and create a space where everyone can express themselves freely.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a platform for artists to showcase their work and connect with their audience. We believe that music has the power to bring people together and create positive change in the world. We are committed to supporting emerging artists and providing them with the resources they need to succeed.

Our Story

Materials was founded in 2018 by a group of friends who shared a love for music and a desire to create a community of artists and fans. Since then, we have grown into a diverse and inclusive collective that celebrates music in all its forms. We host events, release music, and collaborate with artists from around the world.
